Krystlelyn Leano-Apostol PT, DPT

I am a licensed physical therapist with a specialization in vestibular rehabilitation. I received my Doctor of Physical Therapy degree from Shenandoah University and have been practicing since 2011.

I am passionate about helping individuals with vestibular disorders regain their balance and improve their quality of life. I have received advanced training in vestibular assessment and treatment techniques, and I am dedicated to staying current on the latest research and best practices in the field.

In my treatment sessions, I take a comprehensive approach to address the unique needs and goals of each patient. This may include exercises to improve balance and coordination, education on vestibular rehabilitation and self-management strategies, and strategies to manage dizziness and other vestibular symptoms.

I believe in the power of collaboration and work closely with my patients, as well as their primary care providers and other healthcare professionals, to provide the best possible care.

If you or a loved one is experiencing vestibular symptoms or have been diagnosed with a vestibular disorder, I encourage you to contact me to learn more about how physical therapy can help. I look forward to working with you and helping you achieve your rehabilitation goals.

Vision / Mission

Vision

KAL Physical Therapy is dedicated to becoming the premier provider of vestibular physical therapy services in our community, offering compassionate, evidence-based care to help our patients achieve their goals and improve their quality of life.

Mission

Our mission at KAL Physical Therapy is to provide high-quality, personalized vestibular physical therapy services to individuals with vestibular disorders, utilizing the latest research and technology to help our patients reach their full potential. We strive to create a welcoming, inclusive, and supportive environment for all of our patients, and to work collaboratively with other healthcare professionals to ensure the best possible outcomes for our patients. We are committed to helping our patients achieve their full potential and lead active, healthy lives.
